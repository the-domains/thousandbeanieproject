Thousands remain homeless after devastating Nepal quake

Updated August 16, 2015 07:05:29 Three-and-a-half months have passed since Nepal was devastated by a magnitude-7.9 earthquake that killed more than 8,000 people and left more than 2 million homeless. While there are signs that life is returning to normal for many, thousands of people who lost their homes are still living under tarps.
